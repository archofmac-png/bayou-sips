Frequently Asked

A few quick answers.

How much does it cost?

Bayou Sips offers three packages: The Mini at $175, The Original at $250, and The Deluxe at $400. Each price is a flat rental that includes the self-serve cart, the frozen drink machine, plastic cups, and your flavor choices.

Are the drinks alcoholic?

All Bayou Sips flavors are alcohol-free by default. You are welcome to add your own spirits to create the perfect signature cocktail for your event.

How many flavors can I pick?

The Mini includes one flavor on a single-spout machine. The Original includes two flavors and the Deluxe includes four flavors, both on a double-spout machine.

Do I get a tent?

The Original and the Deluxe both include a white tent for all-day outdoor use. The Mini does not include a tent and is best for indoor or covered outdoor setups.

What kinds of events do you serve?

Weddings, birthday parties, baby and bridal showers, graduations, corporate events, holiday parties, and backyard cookouts of every size.

How do I book?

Choose a package above and click Book & Pay. The calendar opens, you pick an available date, share your flavor and event details, and pay the full rental amount. You’ll get a confirmation email immediately.
